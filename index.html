Backstory

Josh earned a B.A. in Language Arts and Secondary Education from Whitworth University in 2006. Since then he's had all sorts of adventures, from starting a successful improv company to having a son.

Josh worked in fintech operations for almost five years before deciding he wanted to build software instead of managing projects. He made the right choice, having now fallen in love with coding.
